Characters of My Neighbor Totoro

Introduction: The film features a cast of endearing characters, blending human children with whimsical forest spirits, exploring themes of imagination and growth.

Protagonist Characters

Satsuki

The older sister, brave and responsible, who helps care for her family and discovers the magical world.

Voice Actor: Noriko Hidaka

Mei

The younger sister, curious and adventurous, who first encounters Totoro and brings wonder to her sister.

Voice Actor: Chika Sakamoto

Totoro

The friendly forest spirit, a symbol of nature's magic, who befriends the sisters.

Voice Actor: Hitoshi Takagi

Antagonist Characters

Introduction: While not traditional villains, the antagonists represent challenges like illness and the unknown.

Catbus

A mischievous spirit that aids Totoro but can be unpredictable, adding to the adventure's tension.

Voice Actor: Naoya Takahashi
