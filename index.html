My Neighbor Totoro

Highlights

  • A heartwarming story of two sisters discovering magical creatures in rural Japan.
  • Acclaimed for its animation and themes of childhood innocence.
  • Won the Animage Anime Grand Prix in 1988.

Synopsis

When two young sisters, Satsuki and Mei, move to the countryside to be closer to their hospitalized mother, they discover a world of magical creatures, including the friendly forest spirit Totoro. Through their adventures, they learn about wonder, family, and the beauty of nature.
